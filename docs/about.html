Who am I

My name is KwunMing Pang, and I am from Hong Kong. I am prefer to be called Peter but wouldn't mind either way. I am now located in Melbourne to continue my study as a Master student in Computer Science at the University of Melbourne.

I am open to opportunities including but not limited to research opportunities, work opportunities, photo shooting, and tutoring. I am looking for an opportunity for PhD degree if possible, and therefore all research opportunities are welcomed for me.

My current interested fields of research are distributed systems, natural language processing, machie learning, and quantum computing.
