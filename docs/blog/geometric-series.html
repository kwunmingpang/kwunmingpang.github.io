Geometric series

The series

12+14+18+116+… \frac 1 2 + \frac 1 4 + \frac 1 8 + \frac 1 {16} + \dots 21​+41​+81​+161​+…

is an example of geometric series. A geometric series can be written as

graphical representation of the example Source

a+ar+ar2+ar3+… a + ar + ar^2 + ar^3 + \dotsa+ar+ar2+ar3+…

where aaa is the coefficient of each term and rrr is the common ratio. In the example series, a=12a = \frac 1 2a=21​ and r=12r = \frac 1 2r=21​.
